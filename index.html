OUR ESTEEMED STAFF

Restaurant Owner

As the founder of Little Taco Shop,Mercy has a passion for creating a place where good food and great company come together. With over 15 years of experience in the food and hospitality industry, Mercy has worked in every role from busser to head chef, gaining a deep understanding of what it takes to run a successful restaurant. Mercy is committed to using fresh, local ingredients to bring the best possible tacos to the community.

Restaurant Chef

Chef Karen brings her innovative flair and a deep-seated love for Latin American cuisine to the Little Taco Shop kitchen. A graduate of the Culinary Institute of America, Chef Karen has honed her skills in some of the most prestigious kitchens in the country. She is known for her creative specials and for pushing the boundaries of traditional taco flavors while staying true to their roots. Her personal motto is "Tacos are a canvas for endless creativity."

Restaurant Manager

David is the driving force behind the seamless operation of Little Taco Shop. With a background in business management and a true talent for creating a welcoming atmosphere, he ensures every customer has an exceptional dining experience. David is a master of logistics, from coordinating staff to managing inventory, and he works tirelessly to make sure the restaurant runs like a well-oiled machine. He is a firm believer that great service is just as important as great food.

Marketing Director

Sarah is the creative mind responsible for sharing the Little Taco Shop story with the world. With over a decade of experience in digital marketing and brand strategy, she crafts all our online content, manages our social media, and plans all our community events. Sarah is passionate about connecting with customers and building a strong, vibrant brand that reflects the heart of the business. She is a true taco enthusiast and is always looking for new ways to get the word out.

Human Resource Manager

Mark is dedicated to cultivating a positive and supportive work environment for every member of the Little Taco Shop family. He handles all aspects of talent management, from recruiting and training new employees to developing programs that foster team collaboration and professional growth. Mark believes that a happy and motivated staff is the key ingredient to a successful restaurant, and he works to ensure everyone feels valued and respected.

Our Accountant

Jessica is the financial wizard who keeps Little Taco Shop's books in perfect order. With her sharp analytical skills and a meticulous eye for detail, she manages our budgets, payroll, and financial reports. Jessica is a Certified Public Accountant (CPA) and is an expert in helping small businesses thrive. She is passionate about financial transparency and ensuring the business is on a solid foundation for future growth.
